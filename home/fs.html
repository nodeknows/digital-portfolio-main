FS Internship & UB Program

Being apart of Fresno State's Upward Bound Program, we were placed in residential dorms over the summer. Many of us came from a similar background, allowing us to form strong connections very quickly!

We had went to several trips throughout our 6-week stay at Fresno State, including this one in San Diego, California! This picture was taken at a local hotel, a day before we went out to San Diego State and UC San Diego.

Fresno State's Upward Bound Program prioritizes the experience of the learning and the students themselves. In this picture they had thrown a surprise glow-in-the-dark party a week before the the program ended!

Meeting people like me made this experience awesome, interesting, and created a learning experience like no other. It was amazing being at different college campuses, taking Fresno State college classes, and building long-lasting relationships!

I started an internship through the Upward Bound Program within the Athletics & Marketing department, here at Fresno State. It was very rigorous, with heavy usage of software and electronic devices.

Being sent to presentations multiple times a week, we had many learning opportunities and professional development days. This really helped me ease into this internship, my first!

At the Fresno State's Athletics Ticketing Department, their facility was right within this stadium. Their work environment was significantly more peaceful and we learned to control ticket distribution and customer service! Hint: me and my internship partner had been offered a part-time job opportunity here!

Showing up to these internship opportunities were life-changing and it set the course to the career path of technology for me. Being able to understand that research is the true work, it allowed me to create foundations for my work ethic. Being in a professional environment was what set me up for success!
