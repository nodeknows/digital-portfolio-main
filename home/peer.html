Peer Mentoring

Doing Peer Mentoring at Sequoia Middle School was a blessing in disguise, not being a fluent Hmong speaker myself, I didn't know what to expect.

Why Peer Mentoring?

Being here for nearly two years, I fell in love with the idea of growing and building friendly and academic relationships with students. There was absolutely nothing else I wanted but to serve this small, underserved community. To see these middle schoolers succeed was the key to my own success.

My Closing Thoughts

Having fun was part of the journey too, with ups and downs with the handling of middle school students. I absolutely loved working for Fresno Unified School District's Peer Mentoring Program.
