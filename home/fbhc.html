Fresno BHC

As a Jr. Community Health Worker (CHW) at Fresno Building Healthy Communities, I learned about the local health resources and related news to apply and canvass into my neighborhoods to provide these resources.

Internship

Our internship consisted of roughly 25 other high schoolers across the district, lasting 9 months from March to November.

With a rigorous application process, being selected to fulfill the Jr. CHW position, I proudly represented the upbringing and the incredible responsibilties to care for my community.

I wanted to create a change not only for my city's future but go down the paved path those before me; to continue where they left off and set our future generation up for success.
